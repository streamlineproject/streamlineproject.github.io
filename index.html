"Streaming is the future but downloading is comfort"

The filosofy of this company

Streaming online is nice. It is fast, user friendly and simple. Yet what if you want to download the content that you where just watching. This is where we come in. With an easely installed extension you can download all content that you streamed in you browser.

Legal warning

Altho downloading the content that you have legal acces to is not illegal, some content providers might stop you from doing so. There is however one catch. It is most defenetly illegal to spread the content downloaded with this extension. Because this means you have no control over who watches the content anymore after you spread it on the internet. We strongly advise you keep all your downloaded content local.
